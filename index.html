Our DNA

We Don't Just Move Machines. We Move Memories.

Founded in the UK with a deep respect for classic craftsmanship, we specialise in sourcing and reviving iconic motorcycles with timeless soul. Whether it's a barn-find 2-stroke from the 70s or a restored twin-cylinder legend, every bike we touch carries the weight of history and the joy of rediscovery.

We serve a growing community of collectors, riders, and enthusiasts across borders especially those in India who crave authentic British and Japanese classics. Backed by mechanical precision, curated sourcing, and a deep-rooted passion for two-wheel tradition, Torque Tradition is where legacy meets the road.

More Questions?
01

Ownership

Who owns the bike?

Ownership is legally transferred after full payment and before dispatch. You'll receive full documentation to register the vehicle in India.

02

Documents

Registration Papers?

Yes. We provide a complete set of export and ownership documents, including the UK V5C logbook (if available), sales invoice, and shipping details.

03

V5C Logbook

What is it?

The V5C is the UK's official vehicle registration certificate. It proves legal ownership and is equivalent to the Indian RC book.

04

Logistics

How does shipping work?

Once ready, the bike is professionally crated and shipped via sea freight. We partner with experienced logistics providers for secure transport.

05

Tracking

Can I track it?

Yes. Once dispatched, you will receive a tracking number and shipping reference to monitor timelines.

06

Condition

What state are they in?

Each bike is either preserved original or restored. We provide detailed photos and condition reports before you commit.

07

Custom

Can I request a model?

Absolutely. Tell us the make, year, and engine size, and we'll explore options and quote accordingly.

08

Damage

What if it arrives damaged?

We use trusted partners to minimize risk. In rare events of damage, we assist with insurance claims.

09

Pricing

Typical costs?

Prices vary by rarity and condition. Since each case is unique, contact us for an accurate, updated quote.

10

Returns

Cancellations?

Due to international logistics, all sales are final. We ensure thorough communication before purchase.

11

Payment

How do I pay?

Payment is made in phases: deposit first to secure, then final settlement before dispatch.

12

Spares

Need parts in India?

Most spares are available via specialist suppliers. We can guide you to trusted workshops when needed.
